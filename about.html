About Me

Hey, I'm Chris. Technology never fails to fascinate me. While I wasn't crafting malicious scripts at a young age, I surrounded myself with technology quite often. This includes, but is not limited to: hacking computer and console video games, administrating various internet forums, creating websites out of curiosity, learning basic in's and outs of computer systems (XP era) from my Grandpa... all before I drove a car! Outside of school, I've interned with a major security company that has given me real-world exposure and experience.
